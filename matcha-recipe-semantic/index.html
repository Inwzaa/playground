Matcha Latte

Let's refresh and recharge with matcha! This vibrant green beverage is rich in powerful antioxidants that can help support your body's defense against harmful diseases and may also contribute to a healthier, more youthful appearance.

Ingredients

  • 1-2 tablespoons matcha powder
  • 250 ml milk of your choice
  • 60-90 ml hot water
  • 1-2 teaspoons sweetener (optional)
  • ice (for iced latte)

Instructions

  1. Place the 1-2 tablespoon of matcha powder into a bowl or mug (a sieve is highly recommended to remove clumps).
  2. Add water and whisk them together until they turn smooth with no lumps
  3. Add sweetener to you liking (optional)
  4. For a hot latte: gently heat and steam your milk, then pour the steamed milk over the matcha shot.
  5. For an iced latte: Fill a glass with ice. Pour the cold milk over the ice, then gently pour the matcha shot over the milk (or stir it all together)
