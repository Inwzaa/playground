My top lists on GMMTV2026


Arrest And Action

A comedy starring Sky Wongravee and Nani Hirunkit as two lieutenants fresh out of training, fueled by a toxic rivalry. Their competitive fire is hilariously doused when they're handed their first mission together: forget bullets and barracks. They have to go deep undercover as T-Pop idols forced to star as lovers in a wildly popular BL series.


Mr. Kill

A gripping crime-thriller starring DewTee in their anticipated first series together. Dew plays "Mr. Kill," the author of a dark crime-mystery web comic, who becomes the prime suspect in a series of murders, each meticulously staged to match the scenes in his comic. Tee is the driven detective assigned to the impossible case. Forced to collaborate, the detective must dive deep into the writer's twisted imagination, risking everything to uncover the truth: Are they really hunting a killer, or is "Mr. Kill" just playing out his final, bloody scene?
